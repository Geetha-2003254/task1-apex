About Kerala

Kerala is known for its backwaters, lush greenery, and rich cultural heritage. Experience the tranquility of houseboats, the thrill of adventure sports, and the warmth of its people.Kerala, a state on India's tropical Malabar Coast, has nearly 600km of Arabian Sea shoreline. It's known for its palm-lined beaches and backwaters, a network of canals.It is bordered by karnataka to the north and northeast, Tamil Nadu a to the east and south. It is a small state, constituting of only 1 percent of the total area of the country.
